## Outreach Strategy

### Method 1: Direct Email (Best Conversion)
**Subject Line Options:**
- "Q1 2026 Tax Delinquency Intelligence: $X in identified accounts"
- "New Data: [Number] S&P 500 companies with public tax delinquencies"
- "Exclusive: Delinquent taxpayer report for [State] - [Number] accounts"

**Email Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

I've compiled the latest tax delinquency data from [X] state governments, 
identifying [Number] companies with outstanding tax debts totaling $[Amount].

Key highlights from Q1 2026:
- [Number] companies owe >$1M in state taxes
- [Number] S&P 500 companies show distress signals
- [Industry] sector has highest concentration of delinquencies
- New clusters in [Geographic area]

This data is publicly available but scattered across 50+ state websites. 
I've consolidated, cross-referenced, and scored every account.

Would you be interested in seeing a sample report? 

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Method 2: LinkedIn Outreach
1. Find "Head of Acquisitions" / "VP Collections" at target companies
2. Connect with personalized message about delinquent account data
3. Share a teaser report (first page free)
4. Offer full report for purchase

### Method 3: Cold Call Script
```
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Your Company]. 

We publish quarterly intelligence reports on tax delinquent businesses 
across all 50 states. Our latest report found [Number] companies owing 
$[Amount] in unpaid taxes, including [Number] that match your target profile.

Would you like me to send over a sample? The full report is $[Price]."
```

### Method 4: Content Marketing
1. Publish free teaser on LinkedIn/Twitter: "50 Companies That Owe the Most in State Taxes"
2. Gate the full report behind email capture
3. Nurture leads with quarterly updates
4. Convert to paying subscribers
